# Research Workflow

## Context

When working in the personal system, read `D:\Code\Playgroud\docs\workflows\research.md` and `D:\Code\Playgroud\docs\assistant\preferences.md`.

## Process

Before research execution, read `docs/profile/user-model.md`. If research audience, source standard, citation style, output type, or evidence standard is missing, use `preference-intake` before producing durable research artifacts.

Infer the user's real research objective and identify the intended audience, depth, output format, source standard, and acceptance criteria. Use `intent-interviewer` for broad or ambiguous research tasks.

Clarify the research question only when it cannot be inferred from files or the user request. Search current sources when facts may have changed. Prefer papers, official documentation, standards, datasets, and institutional sources.

Separate facts, inference, and uncertainty. Preserve source URLs, local paths, keywords, and follow-up actions.

For literature reviews, formal reports, paper drafts, or publishable text, verify citations as a separate task. Do not invent references. Prefer DOI, publisher pages, arXiv, PubMed, ACL Anthology, IEEE, ACM, standards bodies, datasets, and institution pages. Treat unverified citations as leads, not bibliography entries.

For substantial research, deliver both a usable research artifact and a concise explanation of evidence quality, assumptions, and limitations.

## Output

Create or update a knowledge item under `D:\Code\Playgroud\docs\knowledge\items\` when the result has durable value. Update `docs\knowledge\index.md` when adding a new item.
