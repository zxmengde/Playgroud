# Research Workflow

## Trigger

Use for scientific questions, literature review, papers, PDF reading, source synthesis, citation trails, technical investigation, and research knowledge capture.

## Read

Read `docs/workflows.md` and `docs/profile.md`. For Zotero, PDFs, BibTeX/RIS, DOI lists, or citation libraries, use `literature-zotero-workflow`.

## Act

Clarify the research question only when files and context cannot resolve it. Prefer papers, official documentation, standards, datasets, and institutional sources. Separate facts, inference, and uncertainty.

## Output

Produce source-backed notes, review structure, formal text with evidence boundaries, or a durable research knowledge item.

## Verify

For formal text, verify citations separately. Use DOI, publisher pages, arXiv, PubMed, IEEE, ACM, standards bodies, datasets, or institution pages. Treat unverified citations as leads.
